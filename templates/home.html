About Us

Rendezvous is the annual cultural fest of IIT-Delhi. Started in 1976 by a bunch of enthusiastic IITians with a unique vision to encourage cultural exchange and celebrate the passion within, RDV has grown to become North India's largest college cultural festival, drawing a footfall of over 50,000 from 350 colleges all over the country


Rendezvous is the inimitable product of Delhi's unique lust for life and BRCA's proverbial tendency for excellence. It is a 4 day extravaganza with equal parts of awesomeness in organisation and turnout. Having hosted performers such as Arijit Singh, Shankar Ehsaan Loy, Kay Kay, Farhan Akhtar, Vishal Shekhar, and Hoobastank in the past, Rendezvous 2017 promises to be even bigger and better than ever

What is College Ambassador Program?

The Campus Ambassadors Program (CAP) is the premier publicity program of RDV and provides an opportunity to students from colleges all over India to get involved in the making of North India's largest college fest. Campus Ambassadors serve as the face of Rendezvous in their college and get hands on experience in institute level coordination, fest organization, teamwork, event management and media relations.
